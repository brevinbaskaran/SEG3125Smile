A dental team committed to oral health

Our dentists adopt a personalized approach to ensure that each person receives individualized care.

At Smile Dental, we truly believe that going to the dentist shouldn't be a stressful experience. Whether you're a nervous patient in Ottawa or require extensive dental work, our team is up for any challenge when it comes to your oral health. Our goal is to help you maintain healthy teeth for many years to come, and we're committed to making that happen.

Our Services

Say goodbye to dental anxiety with our sedation dentistry - Our gentle sedation techniques ensure a comfortable and stress-free experience for even the most nervous patients.

Emergency Care

Need emergency dental care? Our team is here to provide immediate attention and care to help relieve pain, manage bleeding, and prevent further damage.

$1500 - $2500 / tooth

Cancer Care

Undergoing cancer treatment? Prioritize oral health with compassionate care. We work with patients & healthcare providers to develop personalized plans.

$2500 / tooth

Orthodontic Care

Transform your smile with personalized orthodontic care. Our team offers braces and clear aligners to boost your confidence and oral health. Achieve the smile you want!

$6000 braces

Oral Surgery

Looking for oral surgery options? Our experienced team offers compassionate care for wisdom teeth removal, dental implants, and more. Trust us for a healthy smile!

$3000 / tooth

Preventative Care

Preventive dental care is the key to maintaining a healthy smile. Regular checkups, cleanings, and fluoride treatments help prevent dental problems. Let our family help yours!

$80 / hour
